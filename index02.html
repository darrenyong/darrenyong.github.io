About Me

Formerly lost in the financial industry, I've taken the dive into tech. I'm a software engineer with a passion for React, Redux and Rails. My greatest sense of accomplishment comes from making things more efficient and I love that I'm able to do that as a software developer. I enjoy learning about new technologies to push myself to think more logically and methodically.

When I'm not in front of a computer, you can find me looking for my next subject to place in front of my camera. I also enjoy playing any games I can get my hands on, video or board, and working on my car!
